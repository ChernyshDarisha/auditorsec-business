## Grant Overview

- **Amount**: $25M over 5 years

- **Timeline**: 18-month application and award process

## Project Summary

### Title
"Quantum-AI Convergence Institute for Enterprise Computing Excellence"

### Abstract
This project establishes a comprehensive research and development institute focused on advancing quantum-AI convergence technologies for enterprise applications. The institute will develop breakthrough quantum machine learning algorithms, fault-tolerant quantum computing systems, and hybrid classical-quantum architectures that enable unprecedented computational advantages for enterprise customers.

### Research Objectives
1. **Quantum Machine Learning**: Develop novel QML algorithms for enterprise applications
2. **Fault-Tolerant Systems**: Create error-corrected quantum computing platforms
3. **Hybrid Architectures**: Design seamless classical-quantum integration
4. **Enterprise Deployment**: Enable production-ready quantum-AI solutions
5. **Workforce Development**: Train next-generation quantum-AI researchers
