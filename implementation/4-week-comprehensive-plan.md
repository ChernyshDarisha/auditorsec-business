## **RISK MANAGEMENT & DEPENDENCIES**

### **Critical Dependencies**
1. **CEO Commitment & Availability** - Without CEO leadership, initiative fails
2. **Customer Participation** - Success stories require customer cooperation
3. **Team Alignment** - All team members must commit to messaging consistency
4. **Board Approval** - Final materials require board sign-off for investor use

### **Risk Mitigation by Category**

#### **High Risk - High Impact**
- **Team Unavailability**: Have backup participants for each role
- **Customer Non-participation**: Over-recruit customers, offer higher incentives
- **Message Rejection**: Validate messaging with market testing before finalization

#### **Medium Risk - Medium Impact**
- **Timeline Delays**: Build 20% buffer into each week's schedule
- **Quality Issues**: Implement peer review and external validation
- **Competitive Changes**: Monitor competitor activities, adjust positioning as needed

#### **Low Risk - High Impact**
- **Technology Issues**: Have backup systems and processes ready
- **External Dependencies**: Minimize reliance on external approvals
- **Communication Gaps**: Over-communicate with daily updates and checkpoints
