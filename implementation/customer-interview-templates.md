# Customer Interview Templates & Framework

## **Customer Success Story Interview Guide**

### **Interview Logistics**
- **Duration**: 45-60 minutes
- **Format**: Video call (record with permission)
- **Participants**: Customer champion + decision maker (if possible)
- **Preparation**: Review customer history, contract details, usage metrics

---

## **Interview Script Template**

### **Opening (5 minutes)**
"Hi [Name], thanks so much for making time today. As I mentioned in my email, we're documenting success stories from our top customers to help other enterprises understand the real-world value of quantum-AI computing. 

Before we start, I'd like to record this conversation so I can capture your insights accurately. We'll only use quotes with your explicit permission. Is that okay with you?

Let's start with some background - can you tell me about your role and your company's business?"

### **Section 1: The Challenge (10 minutes)**

**Questions:**
1. "What specific business challenge or opportunity led you to explore quantum-AI solutions?"
2. "What were you using before AuditORSEC? How was that approach falling short?"
3. "What would have happened if you hadn't solved this problem?"
4. "How long had this been a challenge for your organization?"
5. "What was the financial impact of not having a solution?"

**Probing Follow-ups:**
- "Can you quantify that challenge? (time, money, resources)"
- "Who else was involved in recognizing this problem?"
- "What other solutions did you evaluate?"

### **Section 2: The Solution Discovery (10 minutes)**

**Questions:**
1. "How did you first learn about AuditORSEC?"
2. "What made you decide to explore quantum-AI versus classical solutions?"
3. "What other vendors did you evaluate? How did you compare them?"
4. "What was your decision-making process like?"
5. "Who were the key stakeholders in the decision?"

**Probing Follow-ups:**
- "What specifically differentiated AuditORSEC?"
- "What concerns or objections did you have initially?"
- "How did we address those concerns?"

### **Section 3: Implementation Experience (10 minutes)**

**Questions:**
1. "Walk me through your implementation process with AuditORSEC."
2. "How long did it take to get up and running?"
3. "What kind of support did you need from our team?"
4. "Were there any challenges during implementation?"
5. "How was the user adoption process?"

**Probing Follow-ups:**
- "How did implementation compare to your expectations?"
- "What would you tell other companies about the implementation?"
- "Any lessons learned you'd share?"

### **Section 4: Results & Outcomes (15 minutes) - MOST IMPORTANT**

**Questions:**
1. "Now that you've been using AuditORSEC, what specific results have you achieved?"
2. "Can you quantify the business impact? (cost savings, revenue increase, efficiency gains)"
3. "How do these results compare to your original expectations?"
4. "What's been the most surprising benefit?"
5. "How has this impacted your competitive position?"

**Critical Follow-ups:**
- "Can you put a dollar figure on that improvement?"
- "How much time/resources are you saving?"
- "What's the ROI been so far?"
- "How are you measuring success internally?"

**Metrics to Capture:**
- Processing time improvements (hours â†’ minutes)
- Cost reductions ($ saved annually)  
- Revenue increases (new opportunities, faster decisions)
- Resource efficiency (people freed up for other work)
- Accuracy improvements (error reduction %)
- Time to market improvements

### **Section 5: Future & Recommendation (5 minutes)**

**Questions:**
1. "What are your plans for expanding use of AuditORSEC?"
2. "Would you recommend AuditORSEC to other companies?"
3. "What would you tell a peer considering quantum-AI solutions?"
4. "Any advice for other enterprises starting this journey?"

## **Post-Interview Process**

### **Immediately After (30 minutes)**
1. **Transcribe Key Quotes** - exact language customer used
2. **Quantify All Metrics** - specific numbers mentioned
3. **Identify Permission Level** - what can we use publicly?
4. **Note Follow-up Needs** - additional data, references, approvals

### **Within 24 Hours**
1. **Send Thank You Email** with interview summary
2. **Create Case Study Draft** using template below
3. **Identify Quote Usage** - get customer approval for specific quotes
4. **Schedule Follow-up** if needed for additional details

## **Common Questions & Objections**

### **"We're too busy"**
- Offer flexible timing (early morning, evening, weekend)
- Reduce time commitment (30 minutes instead of 45)
- Increase incentive value
- Position as thought leadership opportunity

### **"Can't share confidential information"**  
- Offer to anonymize the case study
- Focus on general outcomes vs. specific processes
- Get legal/compliance team involved early
- Create non-disclosure agreements if needed

### **"Not ready to be public reference"**
- Start with internal-only case study
- Use for investor materials only initially  
- Build relationship for future public use
- Respect their timeline and requirements
