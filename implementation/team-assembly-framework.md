# Team Assembly & Commitment Framework

## **Task 1: Cross-Functional Team Recruitment**

### **Target Team Composition (5-7 Members)**

#### **Core Leadership Team (Required - 4 members)**
1. **CEO/Founder** 
   - **Role**: Strategic vision and final decision authority
   - **Expertise**: Company vision, investor relations, market positioning
   - **Contribution**: Value proposition validation, competitive positioning
   - **Time Commitment**: 4 hours workshop + 2 hours prep + 2 hours implementation

2. **CTO/Head of Product**
   - **Role**: Technical differentiation and product positioning  
   - **Expertise**: Quantum-AI platform capabilities, technical advantages
   - **Contribution**: Technology messaging, competitive technical advantages
   - **Time Commitment**: 4 hours workshop + 2 hours prep + 1 hour implementation

3. **VP Sales/Head of Sales**
   - **Role**: Market-facing messaging and customer perspective
   - **Expertise**: Customer conversations, competitive objections, deal dynamics
   - **Contribution**: Customer language, competitive positioning, objection handling
   - **Time Commitment**: 4 hours workshop + 2 hours prep + 3 hours implementation

4. **VP Marketing/Marketing Lead**
   - **Role**: Message architecture and brand consistency
   - **Expertise**: Brand positioning, content creation, campaign development
   - **Contribution**: Message framework, brand guidelines, material updates
   - **Time Commitment**: 4 hours workshop + 3 hours prep + 8 hours implementation

#### **Extended Team (Recommended - 2-3 additional members)**
5. **Head of Customer Success**
   - **Role**: Customer value and success story validation
   - **Expertise**: Customer outcomes, retention drivers, expansion triggers
   - **Contribution**: Customer success language, value demonstration
   - **Time Commitment**: 4 hours workshop + 1 hour prep + 2 hours implementation

6. **Head of Business Development/Partnerships**
   - **Role**: Partnership and ecosystem messaging
   - **Expertise**: Partner relationships, ecosystem positioning, channel dynamics
   - **Contribution**: Partnership value props, ecosystem messaging
   - **Time Commitment**: 4 hours workshop + 1 hour prep + 1 hour implementation

7. **Head of Finance/Operations** (Optional but valuable)
   - **Role**: Business model and metrics validation
   - **Expertise**: Unit economics, financial modeling, operational metrics
   - **Contribution**: Business model clarity, metrics validation
   - **Time Commitment**: 2 hours workshop participation + 1 hour prep

### **Team Assembly Process**

#### **Week 1: Recruitment & Commitment**
**Day 1-2: Leadership Alignment**
- CEO sends commitment request to core team
- Schedule individual 15-minute alignment calls
- Confirm workshop importance and expected outcomes

**Day 3-4: Extended Team Outreach**  
- Department heads recruit their representatives
- Confirm extended team availability and commitment
- Finalize team roster

**Day 5-7: Logistics Coordination**
- Poll team for workshop scheduling preferences
- Confirm final team composition
- Distribute preparation materials

#### **Team Assembly Success Criteria**
- [ ] 100% core team (4 members) committed
- [ ] 75%+ extended team (2-3 members) committed  
- [ ] All members confirm workshop availability
- [ ] Signed commitment forms from all participants
- [ ] Clear role definitions and expectations set

## **Task 2: Messaging Workshop Scheduling & Logistics**

### **Workshop Scheduling Framework**

#### **Option 1: Single Intensive Day (Recommended)**
**Format**: One 6-hour day with breaks
**Schedule**: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
**Advantages**: Maximum focus, faster resolution, easier scheduling
**Best for**: Co-located teams, urgent timeline

**Sample Schedule:**
- 9:00-10:30 AM: Current state analysis
- 10:30-10:45 AM: Break  
- 10:45-12:15 PM: Value proposition development
- 12:15-1:15 PM: Working lunch
- 1:15-2:45 PM: Message architecture creation
- 2:45-3:00 PM: Break
- 3:00-4:00 PM: Implementation planning

#### **Option 2: Two Half-Day Sessions**
**Format**: Two 3-hour sessions, 1 week apart
**Schedule**: Session 1 + Session 2 (one week later)
**Advantages**: Time for reflection, easier to fit schedules
**Best for**: Remote teams, busy executive schedules

**Session 1 (3 hours)**: Analysis and value prop development
**Session 2 (3 hours)**: Message architecture and implementation

### **Workshop Logistics Checklist**

#### **Location & Technology Requirements**
**For In-Person Workshop:**
- [ ] Conference room for 6-8 people
- [ ] Multiple whiteboards or flip charts
- [ ] Projector/screen for presentations
- [ ] Sticky notes (multiple colors)  
- [ ] Markers, pens, paper
- [ ] Power outlets for laptops
- [ ] Catering arranged (breakfast, lunch, snacks)

**For Virtual Workshop:**
- [ ] Video conferencing platform (Zoom Pro, Teams)
- [ ] Digital whiteboard tool (Miro, Mural, Figma)
- [ ] Screen sharing capabilities
- [ ] Breakout room functionality
- [ ] Recording capability (with permission)
- [ ] Backup communication channel (Slack, phone)

#### **Pre-Workshop Material Distribution (1 Week Before)**
**Materials to Send:**
- [ ] Workshop agenda and objectives
- [ ] Current materials package (pitch deck, website copy, sales materials)
- [ ] Individual preparation assignments
- [ ] Recent customer feedback summary
- [ ] Competitive analysis current state
- [ ] Workshop logistics and dial-in information

**Individual Prep Assignments:**
- [ ] CEO: Strategic positioning review
- [ ] CTO: Technical differentiation analysis  
- [ ] Sales: Customer conversation insights
- [ ] Marketing: Message audit and competitive review
- [ ] Customer Success: Value realization examples
