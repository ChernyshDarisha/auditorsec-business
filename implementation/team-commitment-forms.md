### **Commitment Quality Assessment**
```

☐ Enthusiastic - Sees high value, prioritizing accordingly

```

### **Commitment Reinforcement Process**
**For High-Quality Commitments:**
- Send confirmation and appreciation
- Provide early access to prep materials
- Schedule individual prep check-ins

**For Moderate Commitments:**  
- Schedule 1:1 discussion about value and importance
- Address specific concerns or obstacles
- Provide additional context on workshop ROI

**For Weak Commitments:**
- Executive escalation (CEO involvement)
- Address resource/priority conflicts
- Consider alternative participation models
- Evaluate replacement if necessary

---

**IMPLEMENTATION NOTES:**

**Timing**: Complete team assembly within 5-7 business days
**Success Criteria**: 100% core team + 67% extended team committed
**Quality Standard**: Average commitment strength of "Professional" or higher
**Contingency**: Have backup participants identified for each role
